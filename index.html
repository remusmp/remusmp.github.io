Summary

I am a researcher in mathematical modeling, advanced process control and optimization. I graduated in Electrical Engineering (Automation and Control) in 2012, and obtained my PhD in 2016 at Technical University of Denmark for my research on "Dynamic Modeling, Optimization and Advanced Control for Large Scale Biorefineries". I have a vast experience in multi-disciplinary areas including chemical, biochemical, robotics and maritime technologies, focusing on design and implementation of advanced model-based control algorithms for large scale facilities.
